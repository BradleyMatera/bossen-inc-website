## Technology Stack Recommended
- **Static site:** HTML5, CSS3, vanilla JavaScript (no build step, fast and simple)
- **Hosting:** Cloudflare Pages, Netlify, or Vercel (free tiers, fast CDN)
- **Forms:** Netlify Forms or Formspree for leads; later Formstack/Typeform for complex booking
- **Analytics:** Google Analytics 4 + Google Search Console
- **Call tracking:** CallRail or similar when budget allows
- **CRM:** Simple spreadsheet or HubSpot free CRM; migrate to paid later
- **Email:** Google Workspace for business email
- **Domain:** Register bosseninc.com or similar
- **CDN:** Cloudflare for DNS, SSL, and speed
- **Future:** Calendly/Acuity for appointments, Stripe for deposits, customer portal
