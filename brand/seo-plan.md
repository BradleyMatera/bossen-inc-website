## On-Page SEO
- Unique title tags and meta descriptions for every page
- One H1 per page, descriptive H2s and H3s
- Local modifiers: Rockford, Northern Illinois, I-39, I-90, I-88, I-43
- Schema markup: LocalBusiness, Service, Organization, FAQ
- Internal links between service pages
- Alt text on all images
- Fast load times, mobile-first, accessible forms
- Click-to-call phone numbers

## Structured Data
- LocalBusiness schema with name, address, phone, URL, service area
- Organization schema with USDOT and MC number
- Service schema for each main service
- FAQ schema for verified FAQs

## Local Profiles
- Google Business Profile: claim/verify with exact business name, address, phone
- Bing Places
- Apple Business Connect
- Yelp for Business
- Facebook Business Page
- LinkedIn Company Page
- Industry directories: DAT, Truckstop, 123Loadboard, carrier directories
- Local chamber of commerce and business directories

## Review Strategy
- Request reviews after completed repairs, road calls, parking agreements, and freight deliveries
- Use text/email review request templates
- Respond to positive and negative reviews within 24-48 hours
- Do not write fake reviews or use third-party scores as customer reviews

## Content Strategy
- Answer real driver/fleet questions
- Blog/article subjects: what to tell roadside service, common trailer electrical issues, repair vs rebuild, parking FAQs, preventive maintenance for small fleets, brake/air system warning signs, pre-trip checklist, medium-duty repair considerations, damaged trailer estimate prep, secure parking tips

## Google Business Profile Setup
- Use exact business name: BOSSEN INC
- Primary category: Trucking Company / Truck Repair / Parking Lot (choose best fit)
- Secondary categories: Truck Repair, Truck Stop, Commercial Vehicle Repair, Freight Forwarding
- Add service attributes and products for each service line
- Upload real photos: property, parking, signage, service vehicle, team
- Post weekly updates: parking availability, repair tips, road call status
- Use Q&A to answer common questions
- Set up messaging and call tracking
