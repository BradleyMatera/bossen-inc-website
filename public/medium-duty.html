Who This Service Is For

Medium-duty service is for commercial vehicles that are bigger than a pickup but not a Class 8 semi. We can come to your job site, yard, or roadside location when you need help.

  • Box trucks and delivery trucks
  • Landscape trucks and dump bodies
  • Construction and utility vehicles
  • Small commercial fleets
  • Work trucks used by local businesses

How Medium-Duty Differs from Heavy-Duty

Heavy-duty tractor-trailer service focuses on semi-trucks, trailers, and highway breakdowns. Medium-duty service focuses on lighter commercial vehicles used for local deliveries, trades, and municipal work. The tools, parts, and repair approach can differ, so we ask the right questions when you call.

Common Medium-Duty Work

  • Brake and suspension service
  • Electrical and lighting repairs
  • Engine and drivetrain diagnostics
  • Hydraulic system help
  • Body and bed repairs
  • Preventive maintenance for small fleets
