These are options we can build into a fleet arrangement, subject to confirmation:

  • Scheduled preventive maintenance
  • Recurring DOT inspections and inspection prep
  • Priority repair scheduling
  • Mobile fleet service at your location
  • Trailer maintenance and inspections
  • Repair records and documentation
  • Multi-vehicle service planning
  • Emergency support for fleet breakdowns

How a Fleet Arrangement Works

  1. Review: We look at your fleet, routes, and maintenance needs.
  2. Plan: We create a service schedule that works with your downtime.
  3. Execute: We perform maintenance, inspections, and repairs as planned.
  4. Track: We keep records so you can stay compliant and plan ahead.
  5. Adjust: We refine the plan as your fleet grows or changes.
