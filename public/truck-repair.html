What We Can Handle

BOSSEN INC is set up to work on commercial trucks, trailers, and the systems that keep them moving. These are the repair categories we are equipped to discuss, subject to final confirmation:

  • Brake repair and adjustment
  • Suspension work and alignment
  • Electrical diagnosis and repair
  • Lighting and wiring repair
  • Air system repairs and leak detection
  • Wheel, hub, and bearing service
  • Trailer doors, floors, roofs, and structural repairs
  • Landing gear and coupling components
  • Preventive maintenance programs
  • DOT inspection preparation
  • Welding and fabrication
  • Diagnostic services

How It Works

  1. Call or request service. Tell us the unit, the problem, and where you are.
  2. Inspection. We look at the truck or trailer and identify what needs attention.
  3. Estimate. We provide an honest estimate with parts and labor before work begins.
  4. Repair. We do the work and keep you updated if anything changes.
  5. Pickup. We go over the repair with you and get you back on the road.

Service Types

In-shop repair: Bring the truck or trailer to our location. Best for longer repairs, DOT prep, rebuilds, and welding.

Mobile repair: We come to you for problems that can be safely handled on-site. Learn more about roadside service.
