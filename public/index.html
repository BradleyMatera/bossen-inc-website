Stranded? Here Is What to Do

If you are stuck on the road, the fastest thing you can do is call. We will walk you through the rest.

1

Call us directly

Call the number below and tell us your location, truck/trailer type, and what happened. Do not use the form for emergencies.

2

Describe the problem

No-start, air leak, brake issue, lighting, trailer damage, or something else. The more detail, the better.

3

Stay safe and send photos

Pull off the road safely, turn on hazards, and text photos if you can. We will tell you what we can do from there.
