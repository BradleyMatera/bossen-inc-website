      <h2>Repair vs Rebuild</h2>
      <p>Not every problem needs the same approach. Here is how we think about it:</p>
      <ul>
        <li><strong>Repair:</strong> Fix the specific failed part and get the unit back in service.</li>
        <li><strong>Reconditioning:</strong> Clean, inspect, and restore components to reliable working condition.</li>
        <li><strong>Partial rebuild:</strong> Replace or rebuild a major assembly or section while keeping the rest intact.</li>
        <li><strong>Complete rebuild:</strong> Tear down and rebuild the unit to a fully operational, dependable condition.</li>
        <li><strong>Component replacement:</strong> Swap out worn or damaged assemblies with new, rebuilt, or salvage-quality parts.</li>
        <li><strong>Structural restoration:</strong> Repair or replace frame, panels, flooring, doors, roofs, and other major structures.</li>
      </ul>
